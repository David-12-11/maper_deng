Conference, Meeting and Event Facilities

The perfect venue for any gathering

Pyramid Continental Hotel features modern conference and meeting facilities, with ready access
to state-of-the-art amenities and equipment. The meeting rooms are perfect for various gatherings;
from small social functions, mid-size meetings and conferences to big events, equipped with the latest
audio/visual equipment, broadband Internet services and conference stationery.

Sapphire Board Room

Sapphire Boardroom is configured with the latest high-tech equipment
and capabilities including a wall-mounted LED HDTV with build in video
conference facilities.

Ruby Boardroom

Ruby Boardroom is configured with the latest high-tech equipment
and capabilities including a wall-mounted LED HDTV with build in video
conference facilities.

Diamond Conference Hall

Diamond Conference Hall is ideal for meetings and conferences for smaller groups
for up to 50 delegates. The room is equipped with projector, screen and sound system.

Emerald Conference Hall

Emerald Conference Hall is the perfect venue for larger meeting and events
for up to 120 delegates. The room is equipped with projector, screen and sound system.

Eagle Ballroom

Eagle Ballroom is an elegant venue where you can host larger meetings,
seminars or other memorable events under the crystal chandeliers.
