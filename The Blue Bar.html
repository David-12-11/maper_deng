The Blue Bar in Juba


A Pool Lounge and Aqua Bar designed to offer the most unique experience
to guests staying with Pyramid Continental Hotel, Juba. It is a place for
the discerning and health conscious; seeking to rejuvenate and revitalize
while enjoying a meticulously prepared healthy meal and freshly squeezed
juices with the sights and sunshine of beautiful Juba Skies within the vicinity
of our state of the art Gym and Spa facilities . For when you really just need
to lie back and let yourself relax and drift away, the Blue Bar at the Pyramid
Continental Hotel, Juba is the place to be. Take a dip or lie on a sun lounger,
it's the place that simply washes the stresses away. .
